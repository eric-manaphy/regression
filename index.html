How to use this tool

  • Enter TSV data into the left text area. Wait for the tool to load, and a list of regression models will appear on the left side. Click on one to see how to format your input. You can select one of your independent variables to be processed into a graph from here. It is recommended that the first line of your data is headers, but it is not required.
  • Once you click on a model, you will be prompted to enter in initial guesses. It takes a little bit after your first time hitting submit, but subsequent regressions should be instantaneous as long as you do not leave the site. If it doesn't work the first time, click it again. If it doesn't work after that, refresh and then try again.
